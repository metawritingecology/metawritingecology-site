# Public Record Anchors

Public records are citation and traversal surfaces for Meta-Writing Ecology.

They help readers, citation systems, crawlers, and archives find public materials. They do not replace the internal archive, registry authority, private working corpus, or unpublished system layers.

## Public Anchor Categories

- DOI / publication records listed on [Publications and DOI Records](/publications/)
- OSF project records linked from public publication pages
- The [GitHub Concept Index](https://github.com/metawritingecology/meta-writing-ecology)
- Current OSF document records including [Origin Control and Validity Burden](https://doi.org/10.17605/OSF.IO/28HNK) and [Policy Continuity Evidence Mapping](https://doi.org/10.17605/OSF.IO/QC6GN)
- Figshare and Zenodo mirrors already listed on the publications page
- The [Amazon Author Page](https://amazon.com/author/metawritingecology) and Kindle fiction surface
- Public platform links gathered on [Platforms](/platforms/)

## Boundary

A public record can support discovery, citation, and persistence.

It should not be treated as the complete archive, complete registry, full authority map, or full internal method.
