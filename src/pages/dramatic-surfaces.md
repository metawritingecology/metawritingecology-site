# Dramatic Surfaces

Some narrative materials within Meta-Writing Ecology may move toward dramatic or screenwriting-oriented forms.

These surfaces do not function as model proofs, registry entries, or explanatory keys to the system. They are separate narrative-development layers in which structural pressure is translated through scenes, character relations, institutional conditions, and dramatic progression.

This area may include notes on dramatic form, adaptation logic, scene-based translation, and the movement from narrative structures into performable or screen-oriented writing.

No individual project details are listed here unless they are ready for public release.
