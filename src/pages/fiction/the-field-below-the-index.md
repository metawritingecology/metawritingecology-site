# The Field Below the Index

_The Field Below the Index_ is a seven-part speculative novella cycle about an unnamed Field as it is processed by systems of recognition, praise, supply, translation, strategy, and custody.

_The Field Below the Index_ follows a Field that remains useful to external systems before it is fully recognized by them. Across linked episodes, the book follows procedures of classification, praise, supply, translation, strategy, and custody as they attempt to make the Field legible, portable, and usable. The work can be read as fiction on its own.
